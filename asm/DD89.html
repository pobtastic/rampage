DD89: Handler: Increase Energy (Eaten Food)
Used by the routine at Handler_EatFood.

DD8C LD (IY+$0C),A
Ensure the maximum value for monster energy is not breached.
DD8F CP $41 Return if the monsters energy is lower than 41.
DD91 RET C
